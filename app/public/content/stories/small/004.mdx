There is a <u>pig</u> <u>in</u> the <u>pen</u>. The <u>pig</u> is <u>big</u> and pink. <u>His</u> name is Pete. Pete likes to <u>dig</u> <u>in</u> the <u>mud</u>. <u>He</u> <u>gets</u> <u>mud</u> <u>on</u> <u>his</u> nose.

Pete can <u>eat</u> a <u>lot</u>. <u>He</u> likes corn the best. The farmer brings <u>him</u> corn <u>in</u> a <u>pan</u>. Pete runs to <u>get</u> the corn. <u>He</u> eats it <u>all</u> <u>up</u>!

<u>When</u> the <u>sun</u> <u>sets</u>, Pete goes to <u>bed</u>. <u>He</u> <u>has</u> a <u>bed</u> <u>of</u> hay. The hay is <u>soft</u> and warm. Pete is a happy <u>pig</u>. <u>He</u> <u>had</u> a good <u>day</u>.
