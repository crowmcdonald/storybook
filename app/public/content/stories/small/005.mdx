I am <u>in</u> the <u>dark</u> <u>woods</u>. I am <u>far</u> <u>from</u> my <u>home</u>. The <u>day</u> is <u>cold</u>. I do <u>not</u> <u>like</u> to <u>be</u> <u>out</u> at <u>night</u>. <u>Bad</u> <u>mobs</u> <u>come</u> <u>out</u> <u>when</u> the <u>sun</u> <u>goes</u> <u>down</u>.

I am <u>glad</u> to <u>have</u> my <u>new</u> <u>pet</u>. The <u>wolf</u> will <u>help</u> me <u>fight</u> and <u>keep</u> me <u>safe</u>.
