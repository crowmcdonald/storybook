Tom is a <u>boy</u>. <u>He</u> <u>has</u> a <u>dog</u>. The <u>dog</u> is named Max. Max is <u>big</u> and brown. <u>He</u> <u>has</u> a <u>wet</u> nose and a long tail.

Tom and Max like to play. <u>They</u> <u>run</u> <u>in</u> the yard. Tom throws a stick. Max runs to <u>get</u> it. <u>He</u> brings it back to Tom. <u>They</u> play this game a <u>lot</u>.

<u>When</u> <u>they</u> <u>get</u> <u>hot</u>, <u>they</u> <u>sit</u> under a tree. Tom <u>has</u> a <u>cup</u> <u>of</u> water. Max <u>has</u> a bowl. <u>They</u> both drink. The water is cold and good.

<u>Dad</u> calls them <u>in</u>. It is time to <u>eat</u>. Tom <u>gets</u> <u>his</u> dinner. Max <u>gets</u> <u>his</u> <u>too</u>. After dinner, <u>they</u> <u>sit</u> <u>on</u> the <u>rug</u>. Tom reads a book. Max takes a <u>nap</u>. <u>They</u> <u>are</u> best friends.
